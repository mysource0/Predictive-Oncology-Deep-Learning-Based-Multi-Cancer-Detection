Predictive Oncology: Multi-Cancer Detection

This intelligent system uses Deep Learning and Medical Imaging to detect multiple cancer types including Breast, Liver, and Skin cancers. It assists in early diagnosis, clinical decision-making, and risk prediction.

🧠 Deep Learning

Advanced neural networks automatically learn patterns from medical images to detect abnormalities and classify cancer types with high accuracy.

🩺 Cancer Detection

System detects: • Breast (Normal, Benign, Malignant) • Liver (Healthy, Fatty Liver) • Skin (Dermatofibroma, Keratosis)

⚙️ Models Used

• CNN – Custom deep learning model • VGG16 – Feature extraction • ResNet50 – High-performance architecture

📊 Medical Features

Analyzes imaging patterns such as texture, density, and structural irregularities to identify cancerous tissues.

🏥 Clinical Impact

Supports doctors in early detection, reduces diagnosis time, and improves patient survival rates.

🎯 Output

Provides predicted cancer type along with medical suggestions for further diagnosis and treatment.
